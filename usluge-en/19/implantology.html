IMPLANTOLOGY

Irretrievably lost tooth doesn’t have to mean the end of Your shiny smile. Dental implants represent the most contemporary compensations of tooth in whole. The procedure itself of setting dental implant is very simple and doesn’t require a special post-operative treatment. Time between treatment of setting dental implants and treatment of final production of teeth crowns can be filled with some interim prosthetic solution. Implants are made of plasmated titanium, material that is bio-compatible with our tissue and organism doesn’t see it as a foreign body, which means that there isn’t a possibility of rejecting an implant as a foreign body. Because of its bio-compatibility, implants of new generation integrate with bone tissue.

In that manner they give a feeling, looks and functionality of a real tooth. An implant replaces the root of real tooth, and on it is hitched the superstructure which replaces the sanded stump, on which is cemented crown or bridge.Implant can be a solution for lack of one or more teeth. 
There are many advantages in which patients enjoy with dental implants. Not one prosthetic compensation can create a feeling and comfort of a natural tooth which gives dental implant. Because of its perfect functionality, nutrition of patients with implants will be completely the same as for those with natural teeth, and by that a patient is completely sure of himself and self confident.

Dental implants

A loss of tooth is a common phenomenon that happens in the middle of trauma (during a strike), caries, gums diseases or aging. Whatever the reason is, a loss of tooth must be compensated – because of aesthetic and functional reasons. Although there are numerous options of teeth restorations such as dentures or bridges, it has been shown that non of them is as functional as implants. In many cases, implants are the only logical choice to make a complete restoration of teeth functionality and supporting structures.


What is the procedure like of the dental implants installation?
Before installing implants, it is necessary to check the health of Your teeth and gums; if there are signs of disease, it is necessary to rehabilitate the condition. The treatment will be planned after X-ray examination with which is evaluated the quality of bones and checked the anatomy of support structure. Your dentist will introduce You with procedure of implant installation, explain You possible risks and complications and introduce You to post-operative treatment. After installing implants, there is a period of healing, osseointegration, in which the bone permeates the implant. It usually takes from 2 to 6 months. After that period, it is possible to do prosthetic restoration on the implant.

Who can embed dental implants?
Everyone who is missing natural teeth and who doesn’t have a natural possibility for chewing, talking or aesthetical looks is a candidate to get his teeth embedded. Implants can be a solution for difficult or completely disabled wearing a dental prosthesis.
It is important that You have enough bone tissue, that a bone is firm enough, and that You don’t have health issues which could endanger healing (uncontrolled diabetes, chemotherapy after tumor or similar).

 

What are the advantages of implants?

  • Smaller loss of bone tissue
  • Improved functionality
  • Preservation of natural teeth
  • Aesthetics

Installation of implants with superstructure

Implants that we install consist of two parts. First part is integrated in the bone, and the other one is a superstructure which is screwed by external or internal hexagonal connection in the part which is integrated in the bone.

Superstructure – abutment represents a part on which is cemented metal-ceramics or zirconium – ceramics crown. Superstructure imitates sanded tooth. There are flat and angular superstructures because of needs of the slope of future ceramic covers.

Bridges and dentures on dental implants

Fixed bridge
Before the installation of fixed bridge, there is a thorough analysis of the whole situation and the satisfactory bone condition. After fulfilling wanted conditions, six implants are installed and then on them is fixed a bridge made of 12 members. Sometimes, for fixed reconstructions, it is possible to reduce number of implants, and sometimes it is possible to reach for extra bone regenerations and to increase number of implants.


All-on-4
All-on-4 represents an installation of fixed bridge of a whole jaw anchored on 4 implants. A concept is particularly convenient for those patients who don’t have enough bones for classic implant installation in their side and back parts of jaw. With this way of installation, it is possible to fixedly rehabilitate situation of complete lack of teeth, without any extra procedures of bone regenerations. All-on-4 allows production of fast fixed temporary bridges, with which You can get teeth in one day only.

Hybrid dentures
Fixed rehabilitation is not possible in cases when the bone is significantly reduced. In that case, we make hybrid dentures for patients, which represent foundation for installing four implants and installation of super-constructions for denture stabilization.
This denture is very firm, stable and much smaller in regards to classic mobile denture.


Cover denture on implants
In many cases of complete lack of teeth of lower jaw, patients cannot wear classic mobile denture. We recommend them cover denture: in frontal part of lower jaw, we install two implants and on them are set buckles whose main goal is stabilization of jaw and achievement of its complete functionality.

All on 4 &amp; All on 6

All on 4

All on 4 je vrhunski sistem zbrinjavanja potpune bezubosti koji predstavlja izradu fiksnog mosta kompletne vilice sidrenog na 4 implantata. Koncept je naročito pogodan kod pacijenata koji u bočnim, stražnjim dijelovima vilice nemaju dovoljno kosti za klasičnu ugradnju implantata.

Jedna od prednosti All on 4 sistema je da pacijent nakon ugradnje implantata u roku od 24 sata može dobiti funkcionalan fiksiran privremeni rad.

Isto vrijedi i za pacijente koji se odluče za još stabilniji, All on 6 metod, gdje se umjesto 4 ugrađuje 6 implatanta.

Prednosti All on 4 ili All on 6 metode:

  • Zubni implantati doživotno su rješenje
  • Protetska nadoknada dugotrajno je rješenje
  • Rekonstrukcija svih zuba jedne vilice na 4 ili 6 implantata
  • Bez transplantacije kosti i dodatnih zahvata
  • Minimalno vrijeme oporavka
  • Po potrebi, u jednom dolasku obavljaju se zahvati vađenja zuba i ugradnja implantata
  • Poboljšanje kvaliteta života
  • Ugodnost prilikom jela (nema ispadanja proteze prilikom razgovora, žvakanja, kihanja)
  • Higijena (hrana ne upada ispod proteze)
  • Vraćanje prirodnog izgleda lica i izgubljene funkcije žvakanja

Kandidati:

  • Pacijenti koji žele fiksni protetski rad, a izgubili su sve ili većinu svojih zuba
  • Pacijenti koji imaju potpunu mobilnu protezu duži niz godina
  • Pacijenti kod kojih je tokom vremena došlo do teškog gubitka kosti, posebno u zadnjem dijelu vilice.
  • Pacijenti koji imaju zube u jako lošem stanju i koji su predviđeni za vađenje.
Zahvat se ne preporučuje u sljedećim situacijama:
  • Kod uznapredovale osteoporoze
  • Kod pacijenata s nekontrolisanim dijabetesom
  • Kod pacijenata koji su unutar 6 mjeseci bili na terapiji zračenjem u području glave i vrata
